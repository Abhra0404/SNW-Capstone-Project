Our Medical Services

Comprehensive healthcare solutions for all your needs

Primary Care

Comprehensive healthcare services for patients of all ages, focusing on prevention and early detection.

  • Annual health examinations
  • Vaccinations and immunizations
  • Health screenings and check-ups
  • Minor illnesses and injuries
  • Chronic disease management
  • Preventive care counseling

Chronic Disease Management

Specialized care for patients with long-term health conditions to improve quality of life.

  • Diabetes management and education
  • Hypertension monitoring and treatment
  • Asthma and COPD care
  • Arthritis and rheumatic conditions
  • Kidney disease management
  • Medication management

Mental Health Services

Comprehensive mental healthcare with experienced psychiatrists and therapists.

  • Individual counseling and therapy
  • Depression and anxiety treatment
  • Stress management programs
  • Family and couples counseling
  • Addiction treatment services
  • Psychiatric evaluation and medication management

Cardiology Services

Advanced cardiovascular care using the latest diagnostic and treatment technologies.

  • Cardiac stress testing
  • Echocardiography and EKG
  • Heart disease prevention
  • Hypertension management
  • Cardiac rehabilitation
  • Interventional cardiology procedures

Pediatrics

Specialized healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents from birth to age 18.

  • Well-child visits and vaccinations
  • Pediatric emergency care
  • Growth and development monitoring
  • Childhood illness treatment
  • Nutritional counseling
  • School and sports physicals

Women's Health

Comprehensive healthcare services for women at every stage of life.

  • Annual gynecological exams
  • Prenatal and postnatal care
  • Menopause management
  • Breast health and mammography
  • Family planning services
  • Osteoporosis screening and prevention
